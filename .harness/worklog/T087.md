Also updated the job snippet examples in `CLAUDE.md` and `README.md` from `maxRetries: 1` to `maxRetries: 3` so the recommended default is consistent.

## Rationale for using 3 uniformly

All 8 jobs are idempotent via the `work_items` ledger — a retry just re-runs the loop and skips already-done items. Paid calls (Google Places, Gemini, Fragrantica) are gated by service quotas so a retry cannot double-spend. No job had a reason to keep a lower value.
