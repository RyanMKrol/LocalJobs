## Contrast measurement (flag for T308/T309 — do NOT act on this now)
Same finding as the sibling T315 worklog: the low-contrast look is worst in the `sunny-8bit` theme
family's LIGHT mode (`globals.css` ~line 679-687). Measured: `--accent` (`#e8821f`) against `--panel`
(`#fff9ec`) is ~2.6:1 contrast; `--muted` (`#9c7e44`) against `--panel` is ~3.65:1 — both under the
WCAG AA 4.5:1 floor for body text. T308/T309 plan to hardcode the whole dashboard onto exactly this
sunny-8bit+baloo combo, which would make this contrast gap permanent and app-wide. Whoever builds
those tasks should address this contrast gap as part of that work.
