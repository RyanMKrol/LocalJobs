## Overview

`CronBadge`'s "ⓘ" cron-explanation tooltip (`dashboard/app/ui.tsx`) is a `position: absolute`
floating popup, and every ancestor `.panel` box has `overflow: hidden` (`dashboard/app/globals.css:88`).
Whenever the badge sits near the top of a `.panel`, the tooltip has nowhere to expand upward before
hitting the panel's own edge and gets visually chopped off — confirmed on the workflow detail page's
Schedule row and the Workflows list page's Schedule column. Fix it structurally by rendering the
tooltip through a React portal to `document.body`, which escapes the containing panel's clipping
entirely instead of trying to out-`z-index` it (raising `z-index` does not help — an ancestor's
`overflow: hidden` clips regardless of stacking order).
