## Overview
There are three near-identical ~70-line copies of the Claude-CLI spawn/parse helper. This unit collapses two of them (the blessed src/services/claude.ts and projects-sync/claude-repo.ts) into one internal primitive, and fixes the fact that claude-repo.ts reads its timeout at module load and so ignores the dashboard-editable claude-cli timeout.
