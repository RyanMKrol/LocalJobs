## Overview
The dashboard's Input/Output rows (StageIoItemRow in dashboard/app/components/StageIoLists.tsx) only ever show a work item's `detail.name` (or its raw key) — every OTHER field a stage recorded in `detail` (a resolved Google place_id, a rating/type/address, etc.) is silently dropped from view even though it's sitting in the exact same object the row already has. This makes an input row and its paired output row look identical, and makes an unfamiliar raw key (e.g. a Google place_id) impossible to place in context. Generalize the row renderer to surface a handful of labeled, short scalar detail fields beyond `name`, so the row itself proves what a stage actually produced.
