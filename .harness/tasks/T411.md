## Overview

The `tv-recommendations` workflow's only ledger writer, `tv-recs-notify`'s success
`markWorkItem` call, records `{ name, markdown, title, year }` for each newly-notified
show — but the `Recommendation` object it's working from also carries `reason` (why it
was recommended), `genre`, `lens`, and `tmdbRating`, none of which make it into `detail`.
The dashboard's Inputs & Outputs panel (`StageIoPanel`) can only show what's in `detail`,
so today it can't tell you WHY a show was recommended or how it was rated — only its
name/year and a link to the one shared monthly report. Enrich `detail` per the "every
stage's success `detail` must describe what it produced" convention in the root
`CLAUDE.md`.
