## Overview
The perfumes markdown-profile job currently spreads the owner's own personal/owner data (rating, status, date added, ownership, personal longevity/projection/seasons, notes, application spots) across two inconsistently-named frontmatter blocks and two body sections near the bottom of the document, with only 3 of 6 personal fields carrying a personal_ prefix, an always-"owned" status field that never varies, and the owner's own rating on a 1-10 scale while Fragrantica's community_rating is 1-5. This task makes every personal field clearly personal_-prefixed, groups them into one section positioned right after the researched-data table/first section instead of at the bottom, removes the dead status field, and halves the raw Dynamo rating in the data-parsing layer so it lines up with the community scale.
