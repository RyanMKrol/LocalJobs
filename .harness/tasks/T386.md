## Overview

`StageIoPanel` (the decoupled, tabbed Inputs/Outputs display, complete with its new "Overall" tab as of T385) is currently gated to render ONLY for the `stock-digest` workflow (`dashboard/app/workflow-runs/[id]/page.tsx`, the line reading `run?.workflow_name === 'stock-digest' ? <StageIoPanel .../> : <IoPanel .../>`).
