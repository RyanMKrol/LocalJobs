filter). Two parts:
- **Introduce a `skipped` outcome.** Today `work_items` statuses are `success`/`failed`/`ignored`
  (`markWorkItem`/`isWorkItemDone` in `src/db/store.ts`); a soft-stop (e.g. a `QuotaExceededError`

  `success` with empty output (note: places-ingest is a bulk first stage that marks every item
  success at once — see the CLAUDE.md places note). An item should only be `success` when it produced
  a real output; otherwise `skipped`/`failed`.
